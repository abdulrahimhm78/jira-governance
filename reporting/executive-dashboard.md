# Executive Dashboard (Leadership View)

## Purpose
Executive dashboards provide leadership
with a clear, concise view of delivery
health, risks, and trends.

## What to Include
- Progress toward key objectives
- Delivery predictability indicators
- Quality and stability signals
- Key risks and dependencies

## Design Principles
- Focus on trends, not snapshots
- Minimize metrics to what matters
- Provide context and interpretation

## Common Mistakes
- Overloading dashboards with data
- Reporting activity instead of outcomes
- Sharing raw Jira views with executives
