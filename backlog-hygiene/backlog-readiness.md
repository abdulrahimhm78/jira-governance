# Backlog Readiness (Quality Gate)

## Purpose
Backlog readiness ensures work items
are actionable, understood, and ready
for planning and execution.

## Readiness Criteria
- Clear description and intent
- Acceptance criteria defined
- Dependencies identified
- Size appropriate for delivery

## Good Practices
- Refine backlog regularly
- Enforce Definition of Ready
- Remove stale or unclear items

## Common Mistakes
- Carrying outdated backlog items
- Planning with unclear requirements
- Allowing excessive backlog growth
